The Challenge

Lisa, an entrepreneur with a background in fashion retail, had a vision for an online boutique selling sustainable, ethically-made clothing. She was ready to invest $50,000 in building a full e-commerce platform.

Before committing her savings, she wanted to validate whether there was actually demand for sustainable fashion in her target demographic - environmentally-conscious millennials aged 25-35.

The Solution

Phase 1: Market Research & Landing Page (Week 1)

We created a high-converting landing page showcasing the product concept with email signup for early access. Then ran targeted Facebook and Instagram ads to Lisa's ideal customer demographic - environmentally-conscious millennials.

Phase 2: Ad Campaign Analysis (Week 2)

Analyzed ad performance data. Found that while ad impressions were high, click-through rates were low (0.6%) and email signups minimal. Cost per lead was $89 - indicating weak market demand for this specific niche.

The Verdict

We presented Lisa with clear data: The sustainable fashion niche was oversaturated, and her specific target demographic wasn't converting. We recommended she either pivot to a broader market or test with a pre-order model before full development.
