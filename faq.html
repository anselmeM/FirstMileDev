Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our MVP development services, pricing, and validation process.

How much does MVP development cost?

Our MVP development starts at $2,500 for Phase 1 (validation) and $15,000+ for Phase 2 (MVP build). The cost depends on your chosen technology stack, feature complexity, and timeline.

We offer three tiers: Validation ($2,500), MVP Build ($15,000-35,000), and Scale ($50,000+). All prices include full code ownership and a 30-day post-launch support period.

Use our free cost calculator to get an instant estimate based on your specific requirements.

How long does it take to build an MVP?

Phase 1 (validation) takes 2-4 weeks using landing pages and ad campaigns. Phase 2 (MVP build) takes 4-12 weeks depending on complexity.

For example, a simple web app with 5-7 core features typically takes 6-8 weeks. We use agile methodologies with bi-weekly sprints to ensure fast delivery while maintaining quality.

What technology stack do you use?

For Phase 1 (validation), we use no-code tools like Bubble, FlutterFlow, or Webflow + Wized for rapid testing. For Phase 2 (MVP) and Phase 3 (scale), we use the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) or Next.js for custom builds.

We choose the stack based on your specific needs - low-code for speed-to-market, custom for maximum flexibility and scalability.

Do I own the code after the project?

Absolutely. Once the project is fully paid for, you own 100% of the IP, code, design assets, and any third-party integrations.

We provide complete source code with documentation and can assist with hosting migration if needed. We are your partners, not your landlords - our goal is to make you fully self-sufficient.

What is the validation process?

Our validation process starts with a 30-minute discovery call to understand your idea and target market.

We then create a high-converting landing page and run targeted ads (Facebook, Instagram, Google) for 2-4 weeks. We analyze the data to determine if there's real demand before you invest in development.

This typically costs $2,500 and can save you $30,000+ in unnecessary development costs.

How do you handle communication during projects?

We assign a dedicated project manager who serves as your single point of contact. You'll have access to a private Slack channel for quick questions and updates.

We provide bi-weekly video standups to review progress, demonstrate working features, and gather feedback. All project documentation, timelines, and deliverables are tracked in a shared project management tool.

What happens after the MVP launches?

We include 30 days of post-launch support to address any bugs or critical issues.

After that, we offer ongoing maintenance packages starting at $500/month which includes hosting, security updates, feature enhancements, and priority support. We're also happy to train your team on how to use and manage the product.

Can you help with fundraising?

Yes! Our validated MVPs help founders secure Pre-Seed and Seed funding. We've helped clients secure over $2M in combined funding by proving market demand first.

We can also help create pitch deck materials, demo videos, and investor-ready documentation. Many investors now require validation before considering funding proposals.

What industries do you specialize in?

We specialize in B2B SaaS, marketplace platforms, fintech, healthtech, and e-commerce solutions.

Our validation-first approach works particularly well for complex marketplaces and platforms where market demand is uncertain. We've helped over 50 startups validate and build their products across various industries.

Do you offer fixed-price or hourly billing?

We primarily offer fixed-price packages for predictability and budget certainty.

Each phase has a clear scope and deliverable timeline. For ongoing maintenance and support, we offer monthly retainer arrangements. All pricing is transparent with no hidden fees - we provide detailed quotes before any work begins.

How do you ensure code quality?

We follow industry best practices including code reviews, automated testing, and documentation standards.

All our developers are experienced with clean code principles and modern development workflows. We use Git for version control, implement proper security practices, and ensure all code is optimized for performance and scalability.

Can you work with existing code or partially built projects?

Yes, we can audit and continue development on existing projects.

We offer code review services ($500) to assess the current state, identify issues, and provide a roadmap for completion or optimization. Many clients come to us after unsuccessful attempts with other developers - we help clean up and move forward.

What's your success rate with validation?

About 40% of validation projects result in a 'no-build' recommendation - which we consider a success!

Our honest approach saves clients from building products without market demand. Of the 60% that proceed to build, over 80% secure funding or launch successfully. We're proud to tell clients NOT to build when the data doesn't support their idea.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, we sign NDAs for all projects to protect your intellectual property.

Your idea and business details remain completely confidential. We treat every project with the highest level of privacy and professional discretion. We're happy to sign your NDA or provide our standard template.

How do I get started?

Simply book a free 15-minute discovery call through our Calendly link.

We'll discuss your idea, answer any initial questions, and determine which phase is right for you. There's no commitment - we just want to understand your vision and see if we're a good fit. Most clients start with Phase 1 (validation) to test their market before investing in full development.
